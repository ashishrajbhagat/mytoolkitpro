Why Add Margins to a PDF?

  • Print Preparation: Add a wider "gutter" margin for book binding or hole-punching.
  • Annotations: Create extra space in the margins for handwritten notes and comments.
  • Standardization: Ensure consistent margins across documents from different sources.
  • Improved Readability: Increase white space to make dense text easier to read.
