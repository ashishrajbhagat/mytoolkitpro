  • ✔ Targeted Sharing: Extract only the relevant pages before emailing a document to a client.

How to Split Your PDF

  1. Upload: Select the PDF you want to split or drag it into the upload box.
  2. Define Range: Enter the page numbers you want to keep. You can use commas for single pages and hyphens for ranges (e.g., 1, 3, 5-10).
  3. Split: Click the "Split PDF" button to process your request instantly.
  4. Download: Your new, smaller PDF will be ready for download immediately.
