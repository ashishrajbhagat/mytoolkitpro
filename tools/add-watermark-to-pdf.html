Protect your documents or add professional branding with our powerful, browser-based watermarking tool.

Adding a watermark is the most effective way to protect your intellectual property and prevent unauthorized distribution. Whether you need to mark legal files as CONFIDENTIAL, tag a draft as DRAFT, or reinforce your brand by adding a company name, our tool gives you the precision you need.

🔐 Privacy Guarantee: Just like our other tools, all watermarking happens locally in your browser. Your sensitive files are never uploaded to our servers, keeping your data 100% private.

Protect Your Documents with Key Features

🎨 Opacity & Transparency

Fine-tune the transparency of your text to ensure it's visible enough to protect the file, but light enough to keep the document readable.

📐 Custom Rotation & Position

Rotate your watermark to any angle. Use the classic 45-degree diagonal for maximum security across the entire page.

💎 Secure Layering

Choose to place the watermark over or under the content. Stamping it under makes it more permanent and difficult to remove.

When to Watermark Your PDF?

  • ✔ Protect PDF Documents: Labeling contracts as Original, Copy or Confidential.
  • ✔ Content Creators: Protecting eBooks, designs, or whitepapers before sharing them with clients.
  • ✔ Work in Progress: Marking architectural plans or creative drafts as Not for Distribution.
