How to Remove Page Numbers from a PDF

3 Min Read • Updated March 2026

Sometimes, existing page numbers can get in the way. Whether you're merging documents with conflicting numbering or preparing a file for a presentation, you might need a clean slate. This guide shows you how to erase them.

When Should You Remove Page Numbers?

Removing page numbers is useful in several scenarios:

  • ✔
    Before Merging: To avoid having two sets of page numbers in your final document.
  • ✔
    For Presentation: To create a clean, minimalist look for on-screen presentations.
  • ✔
    Redacting Information: To quickly cover up sensitive header or footer text across all pages.

How to Erase Page Numbers Securely

Our tool doesn't technically "delete" the numbers. Instead, it places a small, opaque white box over them, effectively hiding them from view. This is a fast and reliable method that works on any PDF.

  1. Upload Your PDF: Go to the Remove Page Numbers tool and select your document.
  2. Choose the Location: Select the area where the numbers appear (e.g., "Bottom Right").
  3. Apply & Download: Click the "Remove Page Numbers" button. The tool will instantly cover the numbers on every page, and your clean PDF will be ready for download.
